# Prompt immagini iniziali — California

> **Scopo**: stimolare la fantasia e iniziare a vedere il mondo. Sono prompt **esplorativi**, non canonici (il canone visivo trasversale e le schede catalogo sono ancora da scrivere). Una volta generate le immagini, caricale in `web/public/visual/<categoria>/<id>/<file>.jpg` per vederle nel cruscotto.
>
> Modello consigliato: Midjourney v6 / Imagine (Grok) / FLUX. Tutti i prompt sono in inglese — i modelli generativi rendono meglio cosi'. Aggiungi `--ar 16:9` o `--ar 3:4` a piacere.
>
> **Note su tono**: caldo, ironico, vissuto. Riferimenti dichiarati nel canone: Firefly (Serenity), The Expanse, Alien (atmosfera, non aliena). Cargo industriale, non patinato. Mai chrome lucido, mai sci-fi pulito alla Star Trek.

---

## 0. Style sheet base (incollare in testa a ogni prompt)

```
ART STYLE — California novel, exploratory phase:
cinematic film still, gritty hard sci-fi cargo aesthetic, lived-in industrial
spaceship interiors, warm dim ambient lighting (amber + teal practicals),
analog texture, condensation on metal pipes, scratched panels, hand-painted
warning signs, no holographic interfaces, no sleek chrome, no clean Star Trek
look. Visual references: Firefly's Serenity interior, The Expanse Rocinante,
Alien (Nostromo) — but warmer, more familial, less horror.
Color palette: brass, oxidized copper, faded denim blue, off-white plastic,
deep shadow. Grain: 35mm film, slight halation.
```

## 1. Mood board / atmosfera generale

### 1.1 La California — esterno
```
[STYLE BASE]
WIDE EXTERIOR SHOT of an 80-meter mid-class cargo spaceship called "California",
drifting in deep space near a small orange star. Functional industrial design,
not beautiful: exposed thrust nozzles, cargo container racks, mismatched hull
plates patched over decades. Hand-painted name "CALIFORNIA" on the bow in
faded white block letters. No glamour, no symmetry. A working ship.
NEGATIVE: chrome, sleek, futuristic, military, organic alien curves, weapons.
```

### 1.2 Corridoio interno — atmosfera
```
[STYLE BASE]
TIGHT NARROW CORRIDOR inside the California cargo ship. Low ceiling, exposed
pipes overhead with condensation dripping, cable bundles strapped along the
walls, amber emergency lights glowing softly, a single yellow stripe painted
on the floor. The smell of metal and refrigerant is almost visible. No people,
just the loneliness of a working ship in transit. Lens: 35mm, shallow depth
of field.
NEGATIVE: clean, white, glossy, holograms, transparent surfaces.
```

### 1.3 Cucina / sala comune — cuore della nave
```
[STYLE BASE]
INTERIOR of the California's small communal kitchen / mess hall. Worn metal
table bolted to the floor, four mismatched chairs, a battered espresso pot
on a hot plate, mugs hanging from hooks, a magnetic photo of Earth taped to
the bulkhead, a hand-written note about coffee rationing. Soft warm
lighting, late "ship night". Empty room — just the objects telling the story
of four people who live here for years.
NEGATIVE: modern kitchen, IKEA, bright lighting, futuristic appliances.
```

## 2. Equipaggio — character portraits

### 2.1 Milo (capitano, 40+, capelli grigi)
```
[STYLE BASE]
CHARACTER PORTRAIT, head-and-shoulders. Milo, 40+ years old male, captain
and owner of the California. Salt-and-pepper hair (gray now, dark ten years
ago), warm tired eyes, faint smile lines, two-day stubble. Wearing a faded
denim work shirt with rolled-up sleeves, an old leather strap on the wrist.
Expression: ironic, affectionate, slightly worn. He looks like a man who
tells stories to give meaning to things. Background: out-of-focus warm
ship interior. Cinematic film still.
NEGATIVE: glamour, model-handsome, military uniform, weapons, stern.
```

### 2.2 Vera (pilota e navigatrice, 35)
```
[STYLE BASE]
CHARACTER PORTRAIT, head-and-shoulders. Vera, 35, woman, pilot and navigator
of the California. Sharp focused eyes, dark hair pulled back practical, no
makeup, jaw set. Expression: precise, contained, the kind of person who
answers a feeling with a question. Wearing a charcoal pilot jumpsuit zipped
halfway, a thin chain at the neck. Background: cockpit instruments out of
focus, soft cyan glow on one side of her face. Cinematic, restrained.
NEGATIVE: glamour, sexualized, smiling broadly, military, fancy uniform.
```

### 2.3 Jonas (meccanico, 28)
```
[STYLE BASE]
CHARACTER PORTRAIT, three-quarter shot. Jonas, 28, mechanic of the California.
Open warm face, slight smile, smudge of grease on cheekbone, curly dark hair
tied back loose. Wearing a stained mechanic's coverall opened at the chest
over a faded t-shirt, multitool clipped to belt. Expression: optimistic,
present, the kind of person who talks to the ship like she's a person.
Background: engine room with copper piping and amber work lights. Hands
slightly visible — calloused, capable.
NEGATIVE: clean, glamour, military, stern, dirty in a costume way (real grease).
```

### 2.4 Sam (tuttofare, 23, 4 mesi a bordo)
```
[STYLE BASE]
CHARACTER PORTRAIT, head-and-shoulders. Sam, 23, youngest crew member of the
California, four months aboard. Uncertain eyes, slim build, short hair.
Wearing borrowed work clothes that don't quite fit (sleeves a touch too long).
Standing at the threshold of a doorway — half in, half out — captured mid-
hesitation. Expression: still figuring out where they belong. Soft warm
light from inside the room, cooler light from the corridor behind.
NEGATIVE: confident hero pose, glamour, military, costume.
```

### 2.5 Luce (figlia di Reyes, comparsa cap.5)
```
[STYLE BASE]
CHARACTER PORTRAIT, three-quarter shot. Luce, young woman, daughter of the
late distributor Reyes, met on Kepler-7 colony. Guarded eyes, pragmatic
clothing (worn jacket, no jewelry), short practical hair, carrying the
weight of recent loss but not crushed by it. Expression: distrustful at
first contact, the suggestion of a smile she hasn't shown yet. Background:
warehouse interior on Kepler-7, dim industrial light.
NEGATIVE: glamour, romantic lead, smiling, fragile.
```

## 3. Equipaggio insieme — group dynamic

### 3.1 La cucina, cap.1 (caffe' all'olio motore)
```
[STYLE BASE]
GROUP SCENE, medium-wide. Four people in the small kitchen of the California
cargo ship. Milo (40+, gray hair) leaning on the wall with a mug, telling
a story. Vera (35) at the table, focused on a tablet, half-listening. Jonas
(28) standing by the espresso pot, smiling at something Milo said. Sam (23)
at the threshold, holding a mug with both hands, observing. Warm amber
lighting from a single overhead lamp. Cinematic, late ship-night feel.
The composition reads: family that doesn't say it's family.
NEGATIVE: posed, looking at camera, military formation, action heroes.
```

## 4. Luoghi

### 4.1 Sala macchine (cap.2)
```
[STYLE BASE]
INTERIOR of the California's engine room. Tight space, exposed propulsion
core glowing dull orange behind a safety grate, copper coolant pipes,
analog gauges with hand-written calibration notes, a wrench rack, a small
shrine-like nook with a printed photo and a dried flower. Soft hum implied.
The room smells of ozone and oil. Visual reference: Firefly Serenity
engine room, but smaller and more cluttered.
NEGATIVE: clean, glowing futuristic reactor, transparent panels, holograms.
```

### 4.2 Kepler-7 — esterno colonia
```
[STYLE BASE]
EXTERIOR ESTABLISHING SHOT of Kepler-7, a frontier colony on a dusty world.
Low prefab buildings, rust-stained, antennas, a landing pad with two cargo
ships parked. Orange sky from a small distant sun, dust haze in the
distance. The atmosphere of a place where Sector 8 collapsed three weeks
ago and 23 people died — present in the silence, not declared.
NEGATIVE: bustling city, advanced technology, military presence,
post-apocalyptic ruin (it's worn but functional).
```

### 4.3 Magazzino sopravvissuti — Kepler-7 (cap.7)
```
[STYLE BASE]
INTERIOR WAREHOUSE on Kepler-7. Stacked cargo containers, makeshift cots
along one wall where Sector 8 survivors are sleeping (an older woman, a
man with a scar holding a small girl, a 16-year-old boy). The crew of
the California is unloading 32 containers in the foreground, working
silently. Warm amber work lights, large rolling door open to the dusty
exterior. The shot reads: hard work, quiet sacrifice.
NEGATIVE: dramatic, posed heroism, sentimental staging, clean.
```

## 5. Oggetti-chiave

### 5.1 Il file mp3 (California Love di Tupac, da cui la nave prende il nome)
```
[STYLE BASE]
CLOSE-UP product shot. An old battered audio storage device (small, analog,
20th-century-feel — could be a cracked iPod, a USB stick, a tiny tape
cartridge — your call) labeled in faded handwritten ink "California Love
— Tupac — 1996". Resting on a metal surface next to a half-empty mug.
Warm side light. The object is a relic carried across generations.
NEGATIVE: brand new, glossy, modern smartphone, holographic.
```

### 5.2 Container marijuana (carico ufficiale "materiale organico")
```
[STYLE BASE]
PRODUCT SHOT. Industrial sealed cargo container, 2x2x2 meters, painted
faded green with stenciled label "ORGANIC MATERIAL — AGRO RESEARCH —
TEMP CONTROLLED 18C". Refrigeration vents on the side, a barcode tag, an
official-looking but slightly worn customs sticker. Sitting in a cargo
hold under amber light. Looks completely legitimate. (The reality is
inside.)
NEGATIVE: drugs visible, suspicious, military stencils, futuristic.
```

### 5.3 Foto ingiallita (cap.3, nel cassetto di Vera)
```
[STYLE BASE]
EXTREME CLOSE-UP. A small yellowed photograph held at the edge by two
fingers. The image on the photo is unclear — could be a person, could be
a place, intentionally ambiguous. The paper is worn at the corners,
slightly folded. Warm dim light from a bedside source. Mood: a private
object, weight without explanation.
NEGATIVE: clear subject in photo, dramatic reveal, sepia overdone.
```

## Iterazione

Questi sono prompt esplorativi v1. Una volta generate le prime versioni:

1. Identifica quelle che "fanno scattare" il mondo
2. Ne escono i parametri canonici (palette, lente, atmosfera, tic visivi)
3. Si compone il **canone trasversale visivo** (style sheet + scale) da incollare in tutti i prompt futuri
4. Si aggiornano i prompt per le viste canoniche (fronte, azione, modalita', turnaround) come da `_fasi/04_catalogo/_template_asset/TEMPLATE_prompt_immagine.md`

Le immagini scelte come canone diventano **reference intoccabili** (bump versione per modifiche).
